Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Veniselle ("we," "us," or "this site") handles information when you visit this website or place an order through one of our order forms.

When you submit an order through this site, we (and our order-processing partner) may collect information such as your name, phone number, delivery address, and any other details requested on the order form. We may also automatically collect basic technical information, such as your browser type, device type, and general location, through standard web technologies.

How We Use Your Information

This site contains affiliate links. When you submit an order, your information is shared with the order-processing network and the merchant fulfilling your order, so they can process payment, Cash on Delivery, shipping, and customer service. We may also share information with logistics and delivery partners as needed to complete your order. We do not sell your personal information.

Cookies & Tracking

This site may use cookies or similar technologies to remember basic preferences, measure site performance, and understand how visitors interact with the page. You can usually adjust your browser settings to limit or block cookies.

Data Retention

We retain order-related information for as long as needed to fulfill your order and meet legal, accounting, or reporting obligations.

Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. To make a request, use the contact details below.

Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
